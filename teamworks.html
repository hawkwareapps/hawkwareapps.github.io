TeamWorks Pro

HawkWare TeamWorks Pro is an easy to use, and easy to configure Collaboration application. HawkWare TeamWorks Pro entire application is built on top of its own fully tested API. This gives users the power to extend or customize it to fulfill your collaboration dreams.

Description

HawkWare TeamWorks Pro was intended for users who may not be expert programmers. Right out of the box it provides the necessary tools to keep the ideas and dialog between your team members related to the vault documents they are talking about. Now you can quickly see who is working on what, how much time they spent, how much time they need, and where all your manpower is going.

HAWKWARE TEAMWORKS PRO INCLUDES:

  • VIBRANT DASHBOARDS

    Every member of the team will have access to an overview of the activity and progress of all your vault projects.

  • FLEXIBLE TASKS

    Teams can create and manage tasks for the entire engineering team. Each task can track the history of the vault documents related to them.

  • SIMPLE TIME MANAGEMENT

    HawkWare TeamWorks Pro makes it easy for design teams to manage their time based on tasks to help keep on schedule.

  • BUILT ON ITS OWN FULL-FEATURED API

    HawkWare TeamWorks Pro enables users to fully extend or customize your collaboration efforts seamlessly.

  • SIMPLE PROJECTS AND MILESTONES

    HawkWare TeamWorks Pro makes it easy for teams to track their deliverables through its simple projects nad milestones workflow.

  • OPEN DISCUSSIONS

    HawkWare TeamWorks Pro makes simplifies the dialogue between different team members by enabling open discussions.
