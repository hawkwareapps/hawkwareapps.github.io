XBOM

XBOM is designed to leverage the already-powerful Bill of Materials capabilities found within SOLIDWORKS PDM to fill the gap between SOLIDWORKS PDM and full Product Life Cycle (PLM) Enterprise solutions for BOM Management.

  • Download
Description

XBOM empowers users to build a comprehensive Bill of Materials (BOM) from multiple BOM sources, without incurring the time and expense of implementing a full-blown PLM system. XBOM builds on the flexibility and ease-of-use you already enjoy from SOLIDWORKS PDM, adding new capabilities to combine and reconcile multiple BOM sources into a single, more complete Bill of Materials process. With XBOM it is easy to view, edit, sort & filter your data to print or export only the relevant information, while maintaining full revision and workflow control.

HAWKWARE XBOM INCLUDES:

  • EASY AUTOMATION

    Automate routines so that you can focus on design. Invest in savings to deliver your projects faster. Improve reuse of product design data

  • QUICK COMMUNICATION

    Connect and share data with non-CAD users. Standardize your design as everyone uses the same practices. Improve data quality by making it more reliable and usable.

  • EXTENDED WORKFLOW

    XBOM adopts to your ways of working. Extend your existing capabilities with customized add-ins. Start small, grow big by taking new functionalities into use.

  • DATA LINKING

    Users can link their data from BOM to SOLIDWORKS PDM, BOM BOM to SOLIDWORKS Files, BOM to Excel, and BOM to CSV Files.

  • BOM EDITING

    XBOM users can edit their BOM-Specific Rows, BOM-Specific Property Values, Hide and Promote Rows, Multi-Level BOM Permission Control, Find and Replace Property Values within one interface.

  • OPTIMIZE BOM MANAGEMENT

    XBOM optimizes your BOM management workflow by merging CAD and other BOM data together, locking down BOM content, while enabling CAD design freedom, and keeping track of changes made to the BOM.
